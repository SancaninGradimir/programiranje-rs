# --description--

Možete koristiti različite fraze ili reči za razgovor o temi, kao što su:

`Justifying` znači dati razlog ili objašnjenje za vaše mišljenje ili postupke. Primer je, `I chose to study at the library instead of at home. Justifying my decision, I find it quieter and less distracting, which helps me focus better on my work.`

`Because` koristi se za objašnjavanje razloga nečega. Na primer, `We're updating the website because it's outdated.`

`That is why` To je još jedan način da se navede razlog. To je kao reći, `The server is down. That is why the website is not accessible.`

`Given` se koristi za uspostavljanje činjenice koja je osnova odluke. Na primer, `Given the high demand, we need more people on the team.` ili `We need more people on the team given the high demand.`
