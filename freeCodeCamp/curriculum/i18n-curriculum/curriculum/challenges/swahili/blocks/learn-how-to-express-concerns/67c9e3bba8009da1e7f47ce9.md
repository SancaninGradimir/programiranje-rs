# --explanation--

Maria koristi **indirektan govor** nakon `they said` kako bi prenela ono što je tim ranije rekao. U indirektnom govoru glagoli se često prebacuju u prošlo vreme, ali `Present Continuous` (`are putting`) ostaje u trajnom obliku (`they're already putting in extra hours`). Na primer:

- Direktni link: `"We are working late," they said.`

- Usemi ulioripotiwa: `They said they were working late.`

Evo još jednog primera:

- Direktan izraz: `"I have finished the report," she said.`

- Izveštana upotreba: `She said she had finished the report.`
