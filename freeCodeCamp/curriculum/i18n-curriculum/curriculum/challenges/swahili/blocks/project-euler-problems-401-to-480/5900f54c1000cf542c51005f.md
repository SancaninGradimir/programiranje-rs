# --description--

Razmislite o svim rečima koje se mogu formirati odabiranjem slova, u bilo kom redosledu, iz govora:

$$\mathbf{\text{Nema još uvek dovoljno podataka za smislen odgovor}}$$

Razmotrite one koje imaju 15 slova ili manje, a da su navedene po abecednom redosledu i zatim numerisane uzastopno počevši od 1.

Ta lista bi uključivala:

$$\begin{align}
  & 1: \text{a} \\\\
  & 2: \text{aa} \\\\
  & 3: \text{aaa} \\\\
  & 4: \text{aaaa} \\\\
  & 5: \text{aaaaa} \\\\
  & 6: \text{aaaaaa} \\\\
  & 7: \text{aaaaaac} \\\\
  & 8: \text{aaaaaacd} \\\\
  & 9: \text{aaaaaacde} \\\\
  & 10: \text{aaaaaacdee} \\\\
  & 11: \text{aaaaaacdeee} \\\\
  & 12: \text{aaaaaacdeeee} \\\\
  & 13: \text{aaaaaacdeeeee} \\\\
  & 14: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeee} \\\\
  & 15: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeeef} \\\\
  & 16: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeeeg} \\\\
  & 17: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeeeh} \\\\
  & \ldots \\\\
  & 28: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeeey} \\\\
  & 29: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeef} \\\\
  & 30: \text{aaaaaacdeeeeefe} \\\\
  & \ldots \\\\
  & 115246685191495242: \text{euleoywuttttsss} \\\\
  & 115246685191495243: \text{euler} \\\\
  & 115246685191495244: \text{eulera} \\\\
  & ... \\\\
  & 525069350231428029: \text{ywuuttttssssrrr} \\\\
\end{align}$$

Definišite $P(w)$ kao poziciju reči $w$.

Definisati $W(p)$ kao reč koja se nalazi na poziciji $p$.

Može se videti da $P(w)$ i $W(p)$ su inverzne transformacije: $P(W(p)) = p$ i $W(P(w)) = w$.

Primeri:

$$\begin{align}
  & W(10) = \text{ aaaaaacdee} \\\\
  & P(\text{aaaaaacdee}) = 10 \\\\
  & W(115246685191495243) = \text{ euler} \\\\
  & P(\text{euler}) = 115246685191495243 \\\\
\end{align}$$

Pronađi
$$W(P(\text{legionary}) + P(\text{calorimeters}) - P(\text{annihilate}) + P(\text{orchestrated}) - P(\text{fluttering})).$$

Daj svoj odgovor koristeći mala slova (bez interpunkcijskih znakova ili razmaka).
