`They might missed the deadline due to a system error.`

### --feedback--

Sledeći glagol `might` treba biti osnovna forma glagola.

---

`They might have missed the deadline because of a system error.`

`They might had missed the deadline if they weren't reminded.`

### --feedback--

Nakon `might`, koristimo `have`, a ne `had`.

3

# --explanation--

Modalni glagol `might` prati ga `have` + `Past Participle` da pokaže mogućnost koja je bila u prošlosti. Ova struktura se koristi kada ne znate da li se nešto dogodilo, ali mislite da je to bilo moguće.

- Tačno: `They might have missed the deadline.`

- Nije tačno: `They might has missed...`, `They might missed...`, au `They might had missed...`
