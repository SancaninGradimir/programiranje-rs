## --text--

Koja rečenica koristi `since` u trenutku `Present Perfect Continuous` sa preciznošću?

## --answers--

`I have worked on the project since last week.`

### --feedback--

Ovo je vreme za `Present Perfect Simple`. Glagolski oblik treba biti u vremenu `Present Perfect Continuous` (`have been working`) da pokaže tekuću radnju.

---

`I was testing the program since yesterday.`

### --feedback--

Ovo je vreme za `Past Simple`. Kada je `Present Perfect Continuous`, treba da koristi `have been` umesto `was`.

---

`I have been testing them since the latest software update.`

---

`I will have been working on the project since next month.`

### --feedback--

Vreme za `Future Perfect` nije kompatibilno ovde; `since` se koristi zajedno sa vremenom `Present Perfect Continuous`.
