# --description--

Glagoli vremena je važno raspravljati o tome kada se nešto dogodi.

Funkcija `in` se koristi za mesece, godine, vekove i duge periode. Na primer, `We will update the software in July`, `I started learning coding in 2022`, `I like to work in the morning`.

Polje `on` se koristi za dane i datume. Na primer, `Our team meets on Mondays`, `The coding class is on April 10th`, `I finished the task on the last day`.

Glagol `at` se koristi za određivanje stvarnih vremena dana. Na primer, `Our meeting is at 9 AM`, `I begin coding at 10 AM`, `We have lunch at 12 PM`.
