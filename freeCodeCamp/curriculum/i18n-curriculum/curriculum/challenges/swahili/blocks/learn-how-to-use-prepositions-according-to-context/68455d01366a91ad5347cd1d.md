## --text--

Obavesti svog menadžmenta o novom rasporedu kancelarije. Koja rečenica tačno opisuje lokaciju odeljenja ili sobe?

## --answers--

`The break room is between the IT department and the kitchen.`

---

`The meeting room is between of the lobby and the copy area.`

### --feedback--

`Between of` nije tačna izjava. Trebalo bi da bude `between the lobby and the copy area` samo.

---

`Reception is between the sales team with the HR office.`

### --feedback--

Trebalo bi da koristiš `between ... and ...`, a ne `between ... with ...`

---

`The server room is in between to the hallway and the printer area.`

### --feedback--

Trebalo bi da bude `between the hallway and the printer area` bez `to`.

# --explanation--

`Between ... and ...` koristi se za opisivanje lokacije koja je između dve određene zone.

Ispravna rečenica prati ovu strukturu: `between the IT department and the kitchen`.

Ostali izbori pogrešno koriste veznike ili afiksi, čime ih čine negramatične.
