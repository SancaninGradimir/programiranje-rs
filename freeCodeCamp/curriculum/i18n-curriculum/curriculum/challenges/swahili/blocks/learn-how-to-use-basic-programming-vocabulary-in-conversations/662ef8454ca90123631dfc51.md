## --text--

Koja rečenica pravilno koristi izraz `a lot of`?

## --answers--

`She learned a lot of programming languages.`

---

`He has a lot of careful in his work.`

### --feedback--

Neno `careful` ni kivumishi, si nomino. `A lot of` inapaswa kutumika na nomino.

---

`I have  a lot of idea for the new app.`

### --feedback--

Brojiva imenica `idea` treba da bude u množini kada se koristi uz izraz `a lot of`.

---

`She has a lot of the most experience in our team.`

### --feedback--

Unapolinganishwa sifa, `more` au `most` ni sahihi, si `a lot of`.
