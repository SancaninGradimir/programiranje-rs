# --description--

Kada kažeš `I don't think I've had the chance`, ukazuješ da nikada nisi imao priliku ili događaj da nešto uradiš. To znači da nikada nisi imao mogućnost svedočenja nekog događaja ili susreta sa nekom osobom ranije. Puni oblik ovog izraza je `I do not think I have had the chance`. Primer: `I don't think I've had the chance to visit the museum.` (nije otišao u muzej ili, barem, ne pamtiš da si tamo bio).

Slušaj i popuni prazninu.

# --fillInTheBlank--

## --sentence--

`Tom: Hey, Alice. Do you know Lisa?`

`Alice: No, I don't think I've had the BLANK to meet her. Who's Lisa?`

## --blanks--

`chance`
